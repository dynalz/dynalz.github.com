About Me

First time I got in touch with code (pascal) back in highschool, I loved the power it gave and the possibilities you could have with it. That was exactly when I knew my path was set.
Going to College I quickly got bored with classes so I always would be working on side-projects with friends. Those were the most interesting times where I kept learning new things everyday. From doing small prototypes in 1 weekend, to spending countless nights reverse engineer reading spaguetti/encrypted code trying to unify the meaning of it.

At 2017 I started building businesses together with Miguel and Eduardo. We successfully made millions in revenue with 0 prior experience on how to build a business and a product.
Fast forward to 2021 and we took a stance on blockchain, trying to solve one of the biggest problems of the industry, tracking DEFI funds. The solution is simple, and the problem super complex. How can we, on a fragmented multi chain world, with millions of tokens, thousands of protocols, on a industry with no standards, present all the relevant data back to the user? Well, that's what I'm solving right now at Pulsar Finance.
